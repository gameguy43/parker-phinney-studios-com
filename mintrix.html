Available Now on Spotify, Apple Music, and wherever else you listen!

Recorded in Sofia, Bulgaria at Doli Media Studio and Studio Pekarnata, the score blends western orchestra with unique colors from Bulgarian folk musical tradition.

You'll hear soaring strings and bombastic brass, as well as kaval (a type of flute), tamboura (mandolin-like), and gadulka (sort of looks like a violin but sounds more like a cross between an erhu and a sitar). As well as some fantastic Bulgarian folk singers!

There's whispering, heavy breathing, and "shout-singing."

It's gritty and tactile, floaty and ethereal, steamy and seductive.

The album also features a beautiful original closing credits song by Jocelyn Scofield called "Leave Without Me," performed by Kimberly Woods.

Featured soloists Hristina Beleva (gadulka) and Teodora Atanasova (cello). Score supervision by Luc Herrmann.

About Mintrix

Mintrix and the Elven Figurine is a new fantasy short film by Emmy-winning duo Kimberly Woods and Andrew Jordan.

Mintrix, an enigmatic and savvy adventurer, joins a young elf on a quest to reclaim an ancient elven artifact before a murderous cult can steal it, while a night spent at an inn ignites a deeper bond…
