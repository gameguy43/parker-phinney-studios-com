Concert Vibes

Ha Giang

A piano duet about my experience traveling for 4 days by motorbike through the mountains of the Ha Giang region of northern Vietnam. You'll hear the motorbike engine, me slowly getting comfortable riding, the busy honking of downtown Ha Giang, the bucolic countryside, the climb into the mountains, and the beautiful view from the top. And the ride back. Listen with headphones to better hear the two separate pianos.
